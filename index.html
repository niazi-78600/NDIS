About Us

At NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme), we are committed to providing comprehensive support and services to individuals with disabilities across Australia. Our mission is to empower people with disabilities to achieve their goals and live fulfilling lives by offering tailored assistance and access to a wide range of supports.

Services

Counsellors

Professional support for individuals with disabilities to address emotional, psychological, and mental health challenges.

School Leaver Supports

Tailored assistance and transition planning for students with disabilities transitioning from school to further education, employment, or other post-school options.

Employment Program

Programs and services aimed at facilitating employment opportunities and supporting individuals with disabilities to secure and maintain meaningful employment.

Psychosocial Skills Program

Support and development programs focused on building essential life skills, enhancing social interactions, and managing mental health challenges for individuals with psychosocial disabilities.

Support Work

Assistance provided by support workers to help individuals with disabilities with daily tasks, community participation, and achieving their personal goals.

Day Program Workshops

Structured workshops and activities offered during the day to promote skill development, socialization, and community engagement for individuals with disabilities.
